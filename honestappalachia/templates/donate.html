Why Donate?

Maintaing a secure website incurs a lot of costs, such as:

  • servers to host our websites
  • SSL certificates to encrypt Internet communications
  • computer hardware
  • domain names

At the moment, our wishlist includes more and better-situated servers, which would allow us to make Honest Appalachia a more secure and reliable platform for protecting the anonymity of its users.
